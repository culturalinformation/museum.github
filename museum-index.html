Below is a list of online museum virtual tours and exhibits:

    British Museum, London : Discover the Rosetta Stone, the egyptian mummies, and many more artifacts from across the world - all in the comfort of your home! Click Here to unlock the secrets

    Guggenheim Museum, New York: The Guggenheim Museum’s street-view 360° virtual tour allows one to witness the famous spiral staircase from your couch at home! Discover the incredible Impressionist, Post-Impressionist, Modern, and Contemporary art! Click here to start exploring!

    National Gallery of Art, Washington D.C: This famous American art museum features two online exhibits through Google. The first online tour displays exhibits of American fashion from 1740 to 1895, including many clothes from the colonial and Revolutionary eras. The exhibition is a collection of works from Dutch Baroque painter Johannes Vermeer.

    Musée d’Orsay, Paris: Get a chance to take a look at some of the most renowned French artists including Maunet, Cézanne, and Gaugin. To explore, click here

    National Museum of Modern and Contemporary Art, Seoul: Google’s virtual tour takes you through six floors of contemporary art from Korea and all around the world. Click here to learn more
